# MechPro Experts Build Phases

## Phase 1 - Marketing + Lead Capture (Current)
- Premium website UI (Landing, About, Services, Contact, Login).
- Multi-intent lead capture form UI.
- Mobile responsive layout + brand system.

## Phase 2 - Authentication + RBAC
- NextAuth/JWT integration.
- Role onboarding: Admin, Business, Partner, Claims.
- Protected routes and session guards.

## Phase 3 - Lead Management Core
- Lead creation workflows.
- Customer and vehicle profile entities.
- RC/photos upload plumbing.
- Lead status pipeline and assignment queue.

## Phase 4 - Service Partner Dashboard
- Partner lead inbox.
- Quotation upload and update timeline.
- Acceptance/rejection + ETA commits.

## Phase 5 - Claims, Invoice, Payment
- Claim case records and checklists.
- Approval workflows and settlement logs.
- Invoice generation, payment tracking, reconciliation.

## Phase 6 - Analytics + Reports
- SLA dashboards, partner scorecards.
- Claim TAT, conversion, and revenue reporting.
- Export-ready operational reports.

## Phase 7 - Deployment + Hardening
- CI/CD, environment segregation, secrets handling.
- Production observability and backup strategy.
- Security checks, load checks, and go-live runbook.
